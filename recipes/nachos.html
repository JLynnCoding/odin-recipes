Nachos

Description

A tame, non-spicy version of the classic Mexican dish. These nachos are a perfect appetizer but also a delicious comfort-food entree.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped white onions
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 10 oz cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • Tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. Saute onions in olive oil over medium heat until soft.
  2. Add ground beef, and cook on medium high heat until brown.
  3. Drain black beans, leaving a small amount of starchy liquid.
  4. Add black beans, diced tomatoes, and spices to the skillet, and cook until liquid has been absorbed.
  5. Transfer to baking pan and top with cheese.
  6. Cook for 9 minutes at 350 degrees.
  7. Serve with tortilla chips.
