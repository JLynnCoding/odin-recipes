Unstuffed Cabbage

Description

A traditional Polish dish; this dish can be made "stuffed," but this alternate dish cuts down on prep time. This is a great winter dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 cans tomato and rice soup
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Boil a head of cabbage in 3 inches of water until the cabbage is soft when a fork is stuck in it.
  2. Cook rice per the intructions on the package.
  3. Remove the cabbage and set aside to cool.
  4. Add tomato and rice soup to the pot.
  5. Mix the ground beef with the rice, salt, and pepper.
  6. Roll the ground beef rice mixture into rolls and add to pot to boil.
  7. Shred the cabbage and boil in the pot for at least 30 minutes.
