Chicken Soup

Description

A classic chicken soup recipe that has been passed down for generations, a secret ingredient makes this more tangy than a traditional chicken soup. It's jam-packed with nutrients while still being low calorie. This dish is the perfect comfort food to keep you warm in the winter.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 2 bags carrots, crinkle-cut
  • 3 cups, white onions
  • 2 10oz cans tomato sauce
  • chicken broth
  • 1 lb small pasta of choice (ditalini, small shells, etc.)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Boil chicken for at least 1 hour to make the chicken broth.
  2. Finely chop onions and celery.
  3. Remove chicken from pot and set aside to cool.
  4. Strain broth through collander and mesh strainer to remove bones and other stray pieces.
  5. Place chicken back on stove and add onions, carrots, and celery.
  6. Add tomato sauce.
  7. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  8. Add 1 cup of chicken broth, if needed.
  9. Shred chicken and add to pot. Cook for at least 30 minutes.
  10. Prepare pasta according to the instructions on the package.
